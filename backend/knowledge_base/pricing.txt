TechMart Electronics — Pricing Policy

PRICE DISPLAY
All prices listed on TechMart are in Indian Rupees (INR) and are inclusive of applicable GST, unless stated otherwise on the product page.

PRICE MATCH GUARANTEE
If you find an identical product (same brand, model, and specifications) at a lower price from another authorized retailer within 7 days of your TechMart purchase, we will match the difference. To request a price match:
1. Contact support with a link or screenshot of the competitor's listing
2. The competitor must be an authorized retailer (not a marketplace reseller or auction site)
3. The lower price must be currently active, not an expired promotion

Price matching does not apply to:
- Flash sales, clearance items, or limited-time promotional pricing
- Marketplace sellers on platforms like Amazon or Flipkart
- Bundle deals or products sold with free gifts

EMI OPTIONS
EMI is available on purchases above ₹3,000 through partner banks and card issuers. EMI tenure options (3/6/9/12/18/24 months) and interest rates are determined by the issuing bank, not TechMart. Any EMI-related billing questions should be directed to your card issuer.

DISCOUNTS AND COUPONS
- Coupon codes can only be applied at checkout, not after order placement
- Only one coupon code may be used per order
- Coupons cannot be combined with EMI no-cost offers unless explicitly stated
- TechMart Gift Cards can be combined with one other payment method for the remaining balance

PRICE CHANGES AFTER ORDERING
Prices at the time of order placement are final for that order. If a product's price drops after you've placed an order but before delivery, the lower price does not apply retroactively — a new order would need to be placed separately.

STUDENT DISCOUNT
Students with a valid student ID verified through our partner platform receive 10% off laptops and accessories, applied at checkout after verification.
