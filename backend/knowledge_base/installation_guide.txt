TechMart Electronics — Installation Guide

WHICH PRODUCTS REQUIRE PROFESSIONAL INSTALLATION
The following categories require installation by an authorized TechMart installation partner to remain eligible under warranty:
- Air conditioners (split and window units)
- Refrigerators (side-by-side and French door models above 500L)
- Washing machines (front-load models)
- Wall-mounted TVs above 55 inches
- Modular kitchen appliances (built-in ovens, hobs, chimneys)

Smaller appliances (microwaves, standard TVs under 55 inches, mixers, small kitchen appliances) do not require professional installation and can be self-installed using the included user manual.

SCHEDULING INSTALLATION
For products requiring installation, our installation partner will contact you within 2-3 business days of delivery to schedule a convenient time slot. Installation must be completed within 7 days of delivery to keep your hardware warranty valid.

BEFORE THE INSTALLATION VISIT
- Do not open the product packaging yourself — for warranty-eligible categories, only the delivery associate or installation partner may open the box
- Ensure the installation location (wall space, plumbing point, electrical socket) is ready and accessible
- Keep your order confirmation and ID proof available for verification

DURING INSTALLATION
The technician will:
1. Unbox and inspect the product for shipping damage
2. Install and test the product in its intended location
3. Demonstrate basic operation and settings
4. Provide a job sheet confirming successful installation

If the product is found to be non-working or damaged at the time of installation, the technician will note this on the job sheet, which is required to file a replacement request.

SELF-INSTALLATION PRODUCTS
For products that don't require professional installation, refer to the printed quick-start guide in the box, or download the full user manual PDF from the product page. Most self-installable products also have a setup video linked on their product page.

INSTALLATION CHARGES
Standard installation for eligible categories is included free with delivery. Non-standard installation requirements (e.g. custom wall mounting, additional wiring) may incur separate charges, quoted by the technician before work begins.

RESCHEDULING AN INSTALLATION
Installation appointments can be rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance via "My Orders" or by contacting support.
