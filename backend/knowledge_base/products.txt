TechMart Electronics — Product Categories & Availability

PRODUCT CATEGORIES
TechMart carries the following categories:
- Smartphones & Tablets — major brands including Samsung, Apple, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Realme
- Laptops & Desktops — consumer, gaming, and business lines from Dell, HP, Lenovo, Apple, Asus
- Home Appliances — TVs, refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, microwaves
- Audio & Accessories — headphones, speakers, chargers, cables, cases, power banks
- Gaming — consoles, gaming laptops, peripherals, accessories
- Smart Home — smart plugs, security cameras, smart lighting, voice assistants
- Kitchen Appliances — mixers, air fryers, induction cooktops, water purifiers

STOCK AVAILABILITY
Product pages display real-time stock status:
- "In Stock" — ships within 1 business day
- "Limited Stock" — fewer than 10 units remaining
- "Out of Stock" — customers can opt into a back-in-stock email notification
- "Pre-Order" — available for upcoming product launches, with an estimated ship date shown

PRODUCT COMPARISONS
Use the "Compare" feature on category pages to view up to 4 products side-by-side across specifications, pricing, and customer ratings.

BUNDLE OFFERS
Select products are available as bundles (e.g. laptop + bag + mouse) at a discounted combined price. Bundle items ship together and follow the same return policy as individual products — the full bundle must be returned together to qualify for a refund.

BRAND-EXCLUSIVE LAUNCHES
TechMart periodically hosts exclusive early-access launches for select brands, with early-access windows announced via email to subscribed customers 48 hours before public availability.

DISCONTINUED PRODUCTS
If a product is discontinued while your order is pending, TechMart will notify you and offer either a full refund or a comparable replacement product at no additional cost.

For product availability questions, contact support@techmart.com or use the live chat on the website.
