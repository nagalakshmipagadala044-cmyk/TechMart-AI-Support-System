TechMart Electronics — Refund & Return Policy

RETURN WINDOW
Most products purchased from TechMart are eligible for return within 10 days of delivery. The exact return window for a specific product is shown on its product page, as some categories (see exclusions below) have different terms.

ELIGIBILITY FOR RETURN
A product qualifies for return if:
- It arrived damaged, defective, or different from what was ordered
- It is missing parts or accessories listed in the box contents
- It does not match the description on the product page

Products are NOT eligible for return if:
- The customer changed their mind after unboxing/using the product (no buyer's-remorse returns)
- The item is marked "non-returnable" on its product page
- The product is software, a digital gift card, or a consumable (e.g. printer cartridges)
- The product was damaged after delivery due to misuse

REPLACEMENT VS. REFUND
TechMart operates a replacement-first policy for defective or damaged items, subject to the brand's warranty terms:
- If the same model is available in stock, a replacement will be issued after a quality check
- If the same model is out of stock, a full refund will be issued instead
- Replacement is granted only for verified hardware defects; software-related issues are handled through technical support, not replacement

DAMAGED-ON-ARRIVAL ITEMS
If a product arrives visibly damaged, this must be reported within 48 hours of delivery. Do not activate, install, or use the product before reporting damage — TechMart cannot accept a damage claim once a product has been used or activated.

INSTALLATION-REQUIRED PRODUCTS
For large appliances and TVs that require professional installation, the product packaging must only be opened by an authorized TechMart installation partner. Opening the box yourself before installation may void your eligibility for replacement.

REFUND TIMELINE
Once a returned item passes quality inspection, refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

HOW TO START A RETURN
1. Go to "My Orders" in your account
2. Select the order and choose "Return / Replace"
3. Choose a reason and upload photos if the item arrived damaged
4. A pickup will be scheduled, or you may be asked to visit a TechMart store

For any refund status questions, contact support@techmart.com or call 1-800-555-0199.
