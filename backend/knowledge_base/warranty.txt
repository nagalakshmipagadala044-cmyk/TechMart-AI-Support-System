TechMart Electronics — Warranty Policy

WARRANTY COVERAGE
All products sold on TechMart come with a manufacturer's warranty, the duration of which varies by category:
- Smartphones & tablets: 1 year
- Laptops & desktops: 1 year (parts + labor), extendable to 2 years
- Home appliances (TVs, ACs, refrigerators): 1 year, compressor/panel components may carry extended coverage per brand
- Accessories (chargers, cables, cases): 6 months
- Small kitchen appliances: 1 year

The exact warranty duration and terms for a specific product are listed on its product page, as they are set by the manufacturer, not TechMart.

WHAT'S COVERED
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware component failures under normal use
- Premature battery degradation beyond the manufacturer's stated tolerance

WHAT'S NOT COVERED
- Physical damage (drops, liquid damage, cracked screens)
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Normal wear and tear (e.g. cosmetic scratches)
- Software issues unrelated to hardware failure
- Products with tampered or removed serial numbers

CLAIMING WARRANTY SERVICE
1. Contact TechMart support with your order ID and a description of the issue
2. For hardware issues, you may be asked to visit the nearest TechMart store or an authorized brand service center for a technical diagnosis
3. Once a hardware defect is confirmed by a technician's report, repair, replacement, or refund is offered based on the brand's warranty terms and part/stock availability

EXTENDED WARRANTY
Extended warranty plans are available for purchase at checkout on eligible products (primarily laptops, TVs, and large appliances), extending coverage by an additional 1-2 years beyond the standard manufacturer warranty.

INSTALLATION AND WARRANTY VALIDITY
For products requiring professional installation (TVs, ACs, large appliances), warranty validity requires installation by an authorized TechMart installation partner. Self-installation may void hardware warranty eligibility.

WARRANTY REGISTRATION
Some brands require online warranty registration within 30 days of purchase. Check your product's warranty card or the manufacturer's website for registration requirements.

For warranty claims, contact support@techmart.com, call 1-800-555-0199, or visit your nearest TechMart store.
