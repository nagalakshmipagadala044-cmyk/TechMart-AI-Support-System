TechMart Electronics — Shipping & Delivery Policy

DELIVERY TIMELINES
- Metro cities: 2-4 business days
- Tier 2/3 cities: 4-7 business days
- Remote/rural pin codes: 7-10 business days

Exact delivery estimates are shown at checkout based on your pin code and product availability.

SHIPPING CHARGES
- Orders above ₹999: free standard shipping
- Orders below ₹999: ₹99 shipping fee
- Express delivery (where available): additional ₹149, delivers 1-2 business days faster than standard

ORDER TRACKING
Once an order is dispatched, a tracking link is sent via SMS and email. Tracking codes may take up to 24 hours to activate after dispatch — this is normal and does not indicate a delivery problem.

MISSED DELIVERY
If you miss a delivery attempt, our courier partner will attempt redelivery on the next business day. After 3 failed delivery attempts, the order is returned to the warehouse and a refund is processed automatically.

CANCELLATIONS BEFORE DELIVERY
Orders can be cancelled any time before they are marked "Out for Delivery." Once an order is out for delivery, it cannot be cancelled — you may refuse delivery or initiate a return after the product arrives instead.

STORE PICKUP
For orders placed with in-store pickup, the order must be collected within 48 hours of receiving the pickup-ready notification. A valid photo ID matching the order name is required at pickup. Uncollected orders past 48 hours may be cancelled and refunded automatically.

INSTALLATION SCHEDULING
For appliances and TVs requiring installation, our installation partner will contact you within 2-3 business days of delivery to schedule a visit. Installation must be completed within 7 days of delivery to remain eligible under warranty terms.

DAMAGED PACKAGING AT DELIVERY
If a package arrives with visible external damage, you may refuse delivery, or accept it and report the damage within 48 hours per our Refund Policy.

For delivery issues, contact support@techmart.com or call 1-800-555-0199.
