TechMart Electronics - Frequently Asked Questions

STORE HOURS AND CONTACT
TechMart online support is available 24/7 via live chat. Phone support (1-800-555-0199) is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 9 PM. Physical stores are open Monday to Saturday, 10 AM to 8 PM, and Sunday, 11 AM to 6 PM.

FINDING A STORE
TechMart has 12 stores across major cities. Use the store locator on the website or app to find the nearest branch, along with real-time stock availability at that location.

PAYMENT METHODS
TechMart accepts credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and TechMart Gift Cards. EMI is available on purchases above 3000 rupees through partner banks.

CREATING AN ACCOUNT
Sign up using an email address, or via Google or Apple ID for faster checkout. Account details can be updated anytime under Account Settings.

DATA PRIVACY
TechMart uses industry-standard encryption for all transactions and does not share personal data with third parties without consent. See the Privacy section in Account Settings for full details.
