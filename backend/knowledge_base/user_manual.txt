TechMart Electronics — Account & Platform User Guide

CREATING AND MANAGING YOUR ACCOUNT
- Sign up using email, or via Google/Apple ID for faster checkout
- Update your profile, saved addresses, and payment methods under "Account Settings"
- Enable two-factor authentication under "Security Settings" for added account protection

PLACING AN ORDER
1. Add items to your cart from the product page
2. Review your cart and apply any valid coupon code
3. Select a delivery address and preferred delivery speed
4. Choose a payment method and confirm the order
5. An order confirmation is sent via email and SMS immediately after payment

MANAGING ORDERS
All orders can be viewed and managed under "My Orders":
- Track shipment status and estimated delivery
- Cancel an order (before it's out for delivery)
- Initiate a return or replacement (after delivery, within the return window)
- Download invoices for any past order

WISHLIST AND SAVED ITEMS
Use the heart icon on any product page to save it to your Wishlist. Wishlist items are not reserved — stock and pricing may change before purchase.

NOTIFICATIONS AND ALERTS
Manage email/SMS notification preferences under "Account Settings > Notifications," including:
- Order and delivery updates
- Back-in-stock alerts for wishlisted items
- Price-drop alerts
- Promotional offers (opt-in only)

USING LIVE CHAT SUPPORT
Live chat is available 24/7 via the chat icon on any page. For faster resolution, have your order ID ready before starting a chat.

MOBILE APP FEATURES
The TechMart app (iOS and Android) includes all website features plus:
- Barcode scanner for in-store price checks
- Push notifications for order updates
- Store locator with real-time stock availability at nearby stores

DELETING YOUR ACCOUNT
Account deletion requests can be submitted via "Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account," or by emailing privacy@techmart.com. Order history is retained for legal/tax purposes even after account deletion, per applicable data retention regulations.

For any platform issues, contact support@techmart.com or use in-app live chat.
